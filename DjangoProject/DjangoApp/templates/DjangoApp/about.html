About Love The Music Ltd

Love The Music Ltd is a music performance arts club consisting of like-minded individuals who gather monthly to showcase, develop, and celebrate their artistic talents. The club has been active since 2010, holding monthly meetings for performances and musical piece evaluations.

Club members are encouraged to present musical performances lasting up to 15 minutes. However, all performances must undergo a review process by the committee and be scheduled for a specific date.
